Borden Grants of Rockbridge County

The following dataset is a list of the original Borden grant recipients in Rockbridge and Augusta counties. The dataset on this site was compiled by Larry Spurgeon, who can be reached at lspurgeon333 [at] gmail.com with questions.

Additional resources:

  • Borden Grant Map - georectified map of grants
  • Benjamin Borden: Early Rockbridge Settler - Rockbridge Epilogues
